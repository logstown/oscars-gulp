FAQ

What is this??

Oscars Showdown is a web app that allows users to come together to create their own Oscar-winner-picking competitions. You can create new pools and invite your friends or be invited to a pool yourself. Then make your guesses on the picks page.

Ok, I've made my picks. Now What?

Sit back, relax, and marvel at your well-informed, wise decisions. After that, be sure to come back during the ceremony to view a Live Leaderboard that updates after each oscar is awarded.

There's no 'submit' button on the Picks page. What gives?

Picks are saved as they are clicked, and can be changed right up until the start time of the ceremony. No submitting necessary.
