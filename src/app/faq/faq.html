FAQ

What is this??

Oscars Showdown is a web app that allows users to come together to create their own Oscar award-picking competitions. You can create a new pool and invite your friends or be invited yourself. Then make your guesses on the picks page.

Ok, I've made my picks. Now What?

Sit back, relax, and marvel at your wise decisions. After that, be sure to come back during the ceremony to view a Live Leaderboard that updates after every award.

There's no 'submit' button on the Picks page. What gives?

Picks are saved as they are clicked, and can be changed right up until the start time of the ceremony. No submitting necessary.
